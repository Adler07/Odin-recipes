Lasagna recipe

Description :

  • pasta
  • mincemeat
  • oignons
  • tomatoes
  • cheese

Well it's pretty easy you cook the pasta in a pan and also cut some oignons before putting them in a frying pan with tomato sauce and mincemeat

Then you put the stuff nicely and bake it, idk I never made lasagna, but you should be getting them nice lasagna as a result, congrats :D

Steps

  1. Cook pasta
  2. Cook mincemeat to make the tomato sauce
  3. Put it all together
  4. Add cheese on top
  5. Put it in the oven and let it bake
  6. Here's your good looking food, have a good one !
